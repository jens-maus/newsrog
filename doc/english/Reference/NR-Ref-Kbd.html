Group Window Key Bindings
KeyDescription
! (exclamation) Run action filter.
> (less than) Move to end of article in article viewer.
< (greater than) Move to beginning of article in article viewer.
, (comma) Browse backward.
Pg Up (numeric pad) Browse backward.
^ Change the article list columns.
. (period/full-stop) Browse forward.
Pg Dn (numeric pad) Browse forward.
/ (forward slash) Search through article headers.
= (equal sign) Select sort criteria.
? (question) Search through article in article viewer.
A (upper case) Toggle one author mode.
C (upper case) Copy article list entries to clipboard.
R (upper case) Toggle view of read articles.
S (upper case) Stop pending network operation.
U (upper case) Toggle view of unread articles.
V (upper case) Change display filter view.
c (lower case) Copy article text to clipboard.
d (lower case) Move to next lower thread mode.
k (lower case) Kill by thread or author.
t (lower case) Cycle thread mode.
u (lower case) Move to next higher thread mode.
v (lower case) Change selection filter view.
esc (escape key) Close group window.
7 (numeric pad) Move to beginning of article in article viewer.
1 (numeric pad) Move to end of article in article viewer.
3 (numeric pad) Browse forward.
9 (numeric pad) Browse backward.
2 (numeric pad) Scroll forward in viewer article.
8 (numeric pad) Scroll backward in viewer article.
4 (numeric pad) Scroll left in viewer article.
6 (numeric pad) Scroll right in viewer article.
backspace Move to next higher thread mode.
- (numeric pad) Move to the previous article.
+ (numeric pad) Move to the next article.
( (numeric pad) Move to the previous thread.
) (numeric pad) Move to the next thread.
enter or return Read the current article.
1 (non-keypad) Move the article list window to the front.
2 (non-keypad) Move the article list window to the back.
3 (non-keypad) Move the article view window to the front.
4 (non-keypad) Move the article view window to the back.

- (dash) Change the group list columns.
